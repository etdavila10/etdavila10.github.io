About me

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Eduardo and I am currently a second-year math PhD student at the University of Minnesota. I graduated in May 2020 with a Bachelors of Science in Applied Mathematics and a minor in Computer Science from San Diego State University. While at San Diego State, my main interest was in Algrebraic Combinatorics and in particular Numerical Semigroups and Polytopes which was research conducted under Christopher O'Neill. I also participated in MSRI-UP where I had the opportunity to work with Lie Algebras under Pamela E. Harris. Since starting graduate school.
